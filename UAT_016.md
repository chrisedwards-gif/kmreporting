# UAT 016 — Rota intelligence

Use staging and non-sensitive test figures. Do not add the production RotaCloud key or apply the migrations to production during this test.

## 1. Calibrate a kitchen

1. Sign in as an admin or group manager and open **Rota intelligence → Calibrate site**.
2. Choose Dough Religion.
3. Confirm the labour-productivity target, history depth, planning interval and minimum rest.
4. Review all seven trading-day rules: open/close, prep, close-down, minimum/maximum cover and required skills.
5. Set the day-part curve to **Manual** and make Saturday 17:00 the largest percentage.
6. Save. Confirm a success message and that all values remain after refresh.
7. Change the curve back to **Automatic** and save. Confirm the fallback remains visible and will yield to hourly EPOS history.

## 2. Create private staff constraints

1. Open **Staff profiles**.
2. Add at least one salaried kitchen manager and three hourly team members using UAT-only pay figures.
3. Include different min/target/max hours, preferred days, skill sets and maximum shift lengths.
4. Refresh and confirm profiles persist.
5. Sign in as a Kitchen Manager and confirm the staff-profile page and individual wage data are not accessible.

## 3. Generate and challenge a suggestion

1. Return to **Rota intelligence** and select a future Monday.
2. Add a Saturday event uplift with a reason.
3. Click **Generate rota suggestion** once.
4. Confirm the result shows forecast sales and range, labour budget, suggested loaded cost/hours, backtest accuracy/confidence and unfilled-shift count.
5. Open Saturday's evidence. Confirm its peak and demand source are named.
6. Confirm each day separates total labour budget from controllable budget.
7. Make a required skill impossible to fill and regenerate. Confirm the shift stays visibly unfilled and a warning is shown.
8. Generate again and confirm a new version supersedes the prior suggestion rather than partially overwriting it.

## 4. RotaCloud-safe handoff

1. Download the CSV and confirm date, person, role, start/end, break, paid hours and assignment reason are present.
2. Confirm no individual wage, salary or shift cost appears in the page or CSV.
3. Confirm no shift was created or published in RotaCloud.
4. If a staging-only RotaCloud API key is later supplied, sync the team and verify exact location matching, missing-wage warnings, approved leave and availability before trusting assignments.

## 5. Role, accessibility and failure checks

1. Kitchen Manager: only assigned sites are visible; staff wages and calibration are unavailable.
2. Group manager/admin: all authorised sites and configuration pages are visible.
3. Navigate and generate using keyboard only.
4. Confirm no axe violations in the persona workflow.
5. Temporarily make the save unavailable and confirm no partial plan is retained.

## Pass condition

UAT passes only when the figures are explainable, privacy boundaries hold, impossible cover remains unfilled, the exported suggestion is usable for manual RotaCloud entry, and a manager signs off the operating assumptions for the test kitchen.
